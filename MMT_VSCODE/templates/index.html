About the Project

Why We Built This

Created during our time at VIT Vellore, this tool combines Flask, MongoDB, and Gmail to send smart medication reminders. It's all about saving lives by making sure chronic patients don't forget their meds.

01

Medication Scheduling

Set name, dosage, time and frequency — stored persistently in MongoDB.

02

Email Reminders

APScheduler checks every minute and fires Gmail SMTP reminders at scheduled time.

03

Adherence Dashboard

Live stats: total, taken, missed, and adherence percentage updated in real-time.

04

Confirmation Emails

Instantly sends a styled confirmation when you add a new medication reminder.
